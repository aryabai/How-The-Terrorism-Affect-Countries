TERRORISM OVERVIEW

Geographical Distribution Of Terrorist Attacks



Terrorist attacks are mainly concentrated in South America, the Middle East, Africa, and a small part of Europe. Armed assault, bombing and explosion are the most common used attack type. Terrorist attacks have become more frequent and more widespread from 1998 to the last few years.




Temporal Change In Terrorist Attacks, Deaths And Injuries



The total number of terrorist attacks as well as the number of casualties is increasing year by year.
